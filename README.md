## âœï¸ Customization Guide

### 1. **Update Your Info**
Edit `index.html` and replace:
- Your name (line 33)
- Your title/description
- Your email, GitHub, LinkedIn links (lines 156-157)
- Project details in the Projects section

### 2. **Change Colors**
Edit `style.css`:
- Primary color: `#2563eb` (blue)
- Gradient: `#667eea` to `#764ba2` (purple)
- Accent: `#fbbf24` (yellow)

### 3. **Add Your Resume**
Upload your resume PDF to the repo and update the link in line 40 of `index.html`:
```html
<a href="your-resume.pdf" class="btn btn-secondary">Download Resume</a>
```
